What services do you offer?

I specialize in Web Application Security, API Security, Secure Code Review, Network & Active Directory assessments, IoT security evaluations, and malware/static analysis. No bug bounty services — all work is strictly professional consulting.

How do I engage your services?

Simply reach out through the contact form. I'll schedule a consultation to understand your application, threat model, scope, and timelines, followed by a proposal with pricing and deliverables.

What is your typical turnaround time?

Most web application assessments take 7–10 days. APIs may take 5–7 days depending on documentation. Secure code reviews vary by codebase size. Faster delivery is possible for urgent cases.

Do you work with government organizations?

Yes. I have secured 25+ government platforms across critical sectors. All work follows strict confidentiality and compliance guidelines.

What's included in the final report?

Your report includes an executive summary, risk classification, CVSS scoring, detailed vulnerability breakdowns, proof-of-concept where applicable, and exact remediation steps for developers.

Do you support remediation and retesting?

Yes. I review your fixes, validate patches, and provide a retesting report. I also offer secure code review for teams implementing large-scale changes.

Do you sign NDAs and handle sensitive systems?

Absolutely. All work is confidential. I sign NDAs, MSAs, and follow strict data protection and responsible disclosure practices.

Do you offer CVE research or vulnerability discovery?

Yes. I actively research vulnerabilities and currently have two CVEs pending publication. I can perform targeted research for your tech stack if required.

Are you CERT-In Empanelled?

No — CERT-In empanelment is available only to registered security companies with dedicated 24×7 teams, legal compliance, and multiple certified auditors. Solo consultants or individual researchers cannot apply.

However, I provide high-quality security testing focused on real vulnerabilities, manual analysis, and practical remediation that many teams prefer before going for formal CERT-In audits.

Do your assessments replace CERT-In audits?

No. CERT-In audits can be performed only by empanelled companies for compliance and government mandates. My assessments are designed to prepare you for those audits by identifying real issues earlier, fixing them, and reducing costs during formal compliance checks.

Why choose an independent consultant instead of an audit firm?

Large firms focus on compliance checklists. Independent consultants focus on deep manual testing, real-world exploitation, and uncovering risks automated scanners miss.

You get:

  • Direct access to the person doing the assessment
  • Faster turnaround
  • Real manual pentesting instead of template-driven reports
  • Lower cost compared to big audit companies
  • More flexibility for startups and growing teams

Do you issue compliance certificates?

I provide detailed security assessment reports, risk ratings, and retest verification. For government-mandated compliance certificates (e.g., CERT-In, PCI-DSS), you must go through an officially empanelled audit firm. I can help you prepare for those audits efficiently.
